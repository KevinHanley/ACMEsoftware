FAQ's relating to Courses

arrow_downward

1. What is a course? arrow_downward

1. A course is an opportunity we offer our staff to improve their skillset in many differebt ways.
2. Each course is based around a different topic such as HTML, JAVA, etc.
3. All courses are taught by trained professionals


2. How much does a course cost? arrow_downward

1. All the courses that we offer are free of charge to all employess.
2. We belive that courses provide an opportunity for our staff to grow and learn togther, and that cost shouldn't prevent anyone from that experience.


3. How long do courses last? arrow_downward

1. Each course usually has one class per week, with each class being one hour long.
2. The overall duration varies on a course-by-course bases, but they usually last about 10-15 weeks.
3. Any staff member can join a course even if it has already been running for a number of weeks.


4. Where do these courses take place? arrow_downward

1. All courses take place in our main offices here in Cork.
2. All courses will take place in one of the training rooms on the ground floor.
3. Rooms may vary week-to-week.


5. What is available to learn? arrow_downward

1. Most courses focus on computer languages.
2. HTML, JAVA, PHP, VB, Phyton and more, are very popular.
3. Additionally other topics like data security and database structures are available from time-to-time.


6. What results can I expect? arrow_downward

1. At the end of each course our goal is that you will have a basic or intermediate proficiency in your chosen topic.
2. Furthermore, nearly every courses will reward you will a certificate.
3. Staff members with greater skillsets can transfer between fluidly between projects within the company, therefore increasing their importance in our organisation.

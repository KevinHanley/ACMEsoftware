FAQ's relating to Staff

arrow_downward

1. How does our staff structre work? arrow_downward

1. At ACME Software use a very straight forward system of hierarchy.
2. At the top is the Senior Manager. They control all financial aspects of the company and report to shareholders. They can also be the lead of a project.
3. Beneath the Senoir Manager are Team Leaders. These are senior staff who are each in charge of a project and report all project progress to the senoir manager. They each have several employees who report to them as well.


2. Which staff are allowed to make tasks? arrow_downward

1. All staff members are allowed to create tasks, but only tasks that relate to their specific project.


3. I see a few options that are disabled. What does this mean? arrow_downward

1. Not all employee have access to the same privilages within our Task Management System.
2. Only Team Leaders and the Senior Manager are allowed to add new employees to the system for example.


4. Can I adjust my personal details in "Staff Adjustemts"? arrow_downward

1. No. Only Team Leaders and the Senior Manager are able to access employee data.
2. This option in the menu strip is primary used to re-assign staff to different roles where need
3. To change project send a request email to your team leader. You must have at least two valid reasons to change project.

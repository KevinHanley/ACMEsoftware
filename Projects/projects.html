FAQ's relating to Projects

arrow_downward

1. What is a project? arrow_downward

1. A project refers to the development of software for a customer or consulting with companies in the aid of software development.


2. Who can create projects? arrow_downward

1. Only the senior manager can create new projects.
2. When they create the project they will assign a Team Leader to co-ordinate it.
3. The Team Leader or Senior Manager can then assign staff to work on the project.


3. Can I see the status of a project? arrow_downward

1. Yes. You can check the status of project by navigating to "Projects" -> "View Projects".
2. Here you have the option to view the status of previous projects or ongoing projects.
3. You will be greeted by a screen which lists all your current tasks with the listbox on the left.


4. Can I move to a different project? arrow_downward

1. Generally staff are assigned to a project from beginning to end.
2. But it is possible to change projects, though it isn't something you can do without proir consent.
3. To change project send a request email to your team leader. You must have at least two valid reasons to change project.


5. How long is a project? arrow_downward

1. Each project has a mimimum timespan of 4 weeks.
2. Projects generally last a few short months but can be up to a year long in some cases.


6. Who signs off on the completion of a project? arrow_downward

1. Only the team leader may decided when a project is fully completed.
2. Once completion has been decided on, the senior manager will sign off on the projects completion within the system.
